About Me

I'm a product-focused developer interested in frontend, mobile, and full-stack work. I enjoy building digital products where structure, usability, and visual coherence support each other rather than compete. Long term, I want to shape products, not just implement features.

I started in computer science focused on code and logic, but quickly realized I was more interested in the products built with them. That shift led me toward creative computing and a broader view of software: how it works, how it looks, and how it fits into people's lives.

I care about code quality, visual coherence, interaction clarity, and brand consistency equally. The products I admire most are the ones where these elements come together so naturally that nothing feels accidental.
